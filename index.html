Hi! I'm Peter Flanagan, and I'm a computer science major at the University of Michigan. I was born in rainy Seattle Washington, and moved around until the third grade when I found my home in the suburbs of Philadelphia. I began programming in middle school when my computer applications teacher told me I made a great looking website. That year I submitted a website on Fractal Geometry to the PA State Computer Fair. I won second place at the state finals and was now hooked on computer science.

Since then I have learned a lot and I have improved my web designing skills. I have continued to do some graphic design and have significantly expanded my programming knowledge into other areas. The following is a sample of what I have been up to.
